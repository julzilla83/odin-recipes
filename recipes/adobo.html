Adobo Recipe

Description

This food is a traditional Filipino dish made mainly from pork or chicken meat, cooked with vinegar and soy sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1kg Pork belly
  • 1/2 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup of vineger
  • 1 whole garlic
  • ground pepper
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 5pcs of bay leaves
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a cooking pot.
  2. Add the garlic.
  3. Add the peppercorns and bay leaves.
  4. Put the pork belly in the cooking pot.
  5. Pour the soy sauce and water.
  6. Pour-in the vinegar.
  7. Taste your pork adobo and decide to add salt if needed.
  8. Transfer to a serving plate.
